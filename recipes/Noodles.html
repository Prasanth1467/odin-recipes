Noodles

  • Packet MAGGI 2-Minute Noodles (Masala)
  • 1 Tablespoon Oil
  • 4 Tablespoons MAGGI Rich Tomato Ketchup
  • 2 Tomato (Chopped)
  • 1 Pinch Salt
  1. Boil Water: In a pot, bring 2 cups of water to a boil.
  2. Add Noodles: Once the water is boiling, add the Maggie noodles and cook for about 2 minutes.
  3. Add Seasoning: Stir in the Maggie tastemaker (seasoning packet) and mix well.
  4. Cook Further: Let the noodles cook for an additional 1-2 minutes until they are fully cooked and the water is mostly absorbed.
  5. Serve: Remove from heat, transfer to a bowl, and enjoy your delicious Maggie noodles! Optional: add vegetables or an egg for extra flavor.

Cheesy Scrambled Eggs

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons cheese (grated)
  • 1 tablespoon milk
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon butter
  1. Whisk Eggs: In a bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, salt, and pepper.
  2. Heat Pan: Melt butter in a non-stick pan over medium heat.
  3. Add Eggs: Pour the egg mixture into the pan and let it sit for a few seconds.
  4. Scramble: Gently stir with a spatula until eggs start to set, then add cheese.
  5. Serve: Remove from heat when cooked to your liking. Enjoy on toast or on its own!
